IDR trains and deploys digital risk practitioners, combining academic collaboration with hands-on industry practice to prepare professionals for complex cyber and technology risk challenges.

About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments such as financial services, healthcare and critical infrastructure.

Through partnerships with leading UK universities and collaboration with global industry experts, IDR bridges academic research with real-world projects, ensuring professionals are prepared for evolving technological threats.

Who We Serve

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and experienced practitioners across industries seeking to transition into or advance within digital risk roles.

Key sectors include banking, government, energy, telecommunications, healthcare and technology providers.

Industry & Academic Partnerships

IDR collaborates with universities, regulatory bodies and private-sector organisations to ensure training remains aligned with real-world needs.

Partnerships enable participants to work on live case studies and gain exposure to global regulatory frameworks.
